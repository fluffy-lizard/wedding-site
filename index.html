Our Story

About Us

We met on a rainy autumn afternoon and knew from the first moment that something extraordinary had begun. Over years of mountain hikes, shared meals and quiet evenings, our love grew into something we want to celebrate with the people closest to our hearts.

We chose Uhřínov — a place where the Orlické hory mountains meet the sky — because it captures everything we love: wild nature, quiet beauty and a sense of wonder. We cannot wait to share this day with you.

Questions?

FAQ

What should I wear?

Smart elegant / cocktail attire. Think garden party chic — flowy dresses, linen suits. No white please. The venue is partly outdoors so comfortable shoes are a good idea.

Can I bring my children?

We love children and welcome those of immediate family members. Please let us know in the RSVP so we can arrange appropriate seating and activities.

Is there parking at the venue?

Yes, free parking is available on-site. We also encourage carpooling — use the RSVP message field to connect with other guests.

Will the ceremony be outdoors?

The ceremony is planned outdoors weather permitting, with an elegant indoor alternative ready just in case. This is the mountains — we're prepared for anything!

Can I post photos to social media?

Of course! We'd love to see your captures. Please use the hashtag #LukasAndPaja2027 so we can find them all.

What time should I leave?

The party goes late — whenever you feel ready. Shuttle service back to the recommended hotels will run at midnight and 02:00.
