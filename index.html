Our Story

Two adventurers,
one path

Lukáš and Pája found each other the way mountains find climbers — inevitably. Their shared world is built from steep trails in the Orlické hory, ridge-top bivouacs in the Alps, predawn ski touring starts, and the quiet satisfaction of a summit reached together.

Whether they're clipping into rock faces, pedalling into headwinds on loaded touring bikes, or simply watching evening light change the colour of a ridge, they move through the world as a team — curious, unhurried, and completely at home in wild places.

On July 11th, they'll begin the greatest adventure yet — surrounded by the forested hills they love most.
